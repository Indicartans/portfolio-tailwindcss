Pemapistock

Built With

Laravel Bootstrap MySQL

Pemapistock is a web-based application used to manage the stock of Light Fire Extinguishers (APAR) at PT. Coco Profire Indonesia. This application is designed to simplify the process of recording and monitoring incoming and outgoing goods in real-time.

Equipped with CRUD data features for APAR inventory, tracking of incoming and outgoing goods, and inventory reports, Pemapistock assists warehouse administrators and management in managing the distribution of APAR more efficiently, neatly, and structurally.

With a simple and intuitive interface, Pemapistock minimises the risk of human error and enhances accuracy in managing the company's safety inventory.
